Questions and answers

Clear answers for trade buyers.

These answers cover the practical questions a contractor, retailer, manufacturer or project buyer may ask before starting a quote conversation.

What does MIDX TRADERS LTD supply?

The company deals with wholesale wood and timber, construction materials, sanitary equipment and related materials such as wallpaper, floor coverings and, where relevant, paint or finishing products.

Who are the typical customers?

Typical customers include construction companies, retailers, manufacturers, refurbishment teams and trade buyers who need materials in practical quantities rather than one-off retail shopping.

Can I request a mixed-material quote?

Yes. Many jobs involve several product groups, so enquiries can combine timber, construction supplies, sanitary equipment and related finishing materials in one clear request.

Does the company handle specialist building activities?

The company can discuss specialised building activities, structural steel erection, specialist installations and general building service requirements connected to material supply or project coordination.

What information helps with a faster quote?

Product type, dimensions or specification, estimated quantity, delivery location, preferred timing and any site restrictions will help make the response more useful.
