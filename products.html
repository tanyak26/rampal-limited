Buying notes

Better product detail leads to better follow-up.

For timber, include length, section size, treatment and approximate quantity. For sheet materials, include board type, thickness, sheet size and how many sheets are needed.

For cement and aggregates, include bag counts, bulk bag needs, tonnage if known, access limits and whether the materials are for delivery or collection. If a specification is uncertain, describe the job and the intended use.

Useful request details

  • Timber: treated or untreated, size, length, grade, pack quantity.
  • Sheets: OSB, plywood, MDF or board type, thickness and sheet count.
  • Cement: bag type, bag count, timing and storage/access notes.
  • Aggregates: sharp sand, building sand, ballast, gravel, sub-base or mixed needs.
